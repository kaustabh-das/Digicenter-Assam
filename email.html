IMPORTANCE OF BULK MAILING IN BUSINESS GROWTH AND EXPANSION

The key factor affecting business growth and the need for a business to stay in touch and promote its products and services to its customers or prospects is to do it using an efficient and fast mode of communication. It cannot directly call a customer since it will be a breach of his/her private space and cannot always reach all its customers with one phone call, that is where advertising through email is highly recommended by experts and research agencies.


Email marketing boasts of a higher audience reach which means businesses need to have a smart email marketing strategy to stay ahead of their competitors. Your business also requires creative and innovative Email digital marketing tactics to acquire a larger customer base and hence needs to think on the lines of which services and products would your customers and prospects need. You need to understand customer behavior using various Email marketing tools and email marketing strategies in order to increase customer acquisition rates and profits.


Email marketing is a popular digital marketing tool, According to a study by Direct Marketing Association (DMA) 2018, email marketing statistics show that the $1 you spend on Email Marketing, your ROI (return on Investment) is $32, that is 30% more profits. Another research by Statista (2019) on email marketing shows that by 2022, email users will grow to 4.3 billion, which is a huge scope for any business.
